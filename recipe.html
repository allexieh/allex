🍜 Creamy Sopas 🍜

My Comfort Food

Ingredients

  • 1 cup elbow macaroni
  • 1 chicken breast (shredded)
  • 5 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup evaporated milk
  • 1 carrot (diced)
  • 1/2 cup cabbage (chopped)
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 hotdogs (sliced)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Butter or oil for sautéing

Instructions

  1. Sauté garlic and onion in butter until fragrant.
  2. Add chicken and cook lightly.
  3. Pour in chicken broth and bring to a boil.
  4. Add macaroni and cook until tender.
  5. Add carrots and hotdogs. Simmer 3 minutes.
  6. Pour evaporated milk and stir gently.
  7. Add cabbage, salt, and pepper.
  8. Simmer 2 more minutes then serve warm.

Chef’s Notes

Sopas is best enjoyed piping hot, especially on rainy days or whenever your heart needs a little comfort. Think of those moments when you’re tired from a long, busy day, missing someone you dearly love, or just craving something warm and soothing. It tastes even better when shared with family, friends, or that special someone.

And hey—remember, the way to anyone’s heart is definitely through their stomach! For an extra creamy hug in a bowl, just add a splash more milk.
